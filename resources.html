Resources

Webinars

When COVID forced schools to shift to a virtual platform, UFLI met teachers' needs by providing a free webinar series about how to effectively teach reading online. As we pivot back to in-person learning, the information offered in these webinars can be easily translated back to the classroom. Good literacy instruction is good literacy instruction, regardless of the format!

  Watch the Teaching Reading Online Webinar Series  

Professional Development

UFLI works with schools, school districts, and state agencies in Florida and across the country to provide effective, engaging professional development workshops that will help them understand the science of reading, implement research-based teaching methods, and create a culture of reading. Please contact us to find out how you can receive PD from UFLI.

  Ask us about PD opportunities for your school or district  

Resource Hubs

UFLI has developed several resource hubs to assist teachers and parents find the information they need about reading instruction and intervention, disability legislation as it relates to dyslexia, and tools for working with struggling readers at home and in the classroom.

Digital Apps

UFLI has collaborated with UF's Digital Worlds Institute to create digital versions of our Word Work Mat and Blending Board. These apps can be used for virtual instruction or in classrooms with a smart board or tablets.
